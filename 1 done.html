Jarosław "PashaBiceps" Jarząbkowski

retired Polish professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive and Counter-Strike player born in April 11, 1988

ABOUT PASHA PAPITO

He was part of the famous Polish player group Golden Five, who were arguably one of the most successful teams in Counter-Strike and on top in terms of earnings. Now the Polish CS:GO legend pashaBiceps is all set to debut in combat sports as an MMA fighter. He will be taking part in HIGH League 2, a celebrity combat tournament, on 5th February 2022 at the Tauron Arena in Krakow, Poland.

At 4 years, 5 months and 1 day, pashaBiceps, NEO, TaZ, Snax and byali is the longest-lasting lineup in the history of CS:GO.
